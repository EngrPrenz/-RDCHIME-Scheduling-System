About Our Family Ministry

At JA1 Church, we believe that strong families are the foundation of a strong church and community. Our Family Ministry is dedicated to supporting families at every stage of life, from newlyweds to empty nesters, through biblical teaching, counseling, and community events.

We recognize that every family faces unique challenges, and our goal is to provide the resources, support, and encouragement needed to build Christ-centered homes where family members can thrive in their relationships with God and each other.

What We Offer

Marriage Enrichment

Programs and retreats designed to strengthen marriages through biblical principles, communication skills, and conflict resolution.

Parenting Support

Classes and resources to help parents raise children with strong faith and character in today's challenging world.

Family Counseling

Professional Christian counseling services for families facing challenges, transitions, or crises.

Family Events & Activities

Fun and meaningful events throughout the year that bring families together to build memories and strengthen bonds.
