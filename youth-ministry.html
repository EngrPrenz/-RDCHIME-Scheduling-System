About Our Youth Ministry

The JA1 Church Youth Ministry is dedicated to helping young people grow in their faith and develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. We provide a safe and welcoming environment where youth can explore their faith, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose.

Our ministry focuses on biblical teaching, worship, discipleship, and service, all while creating an atmosphere that's relevant and engaging for today's youth. We believe that young people are not just the church of tomorrow—they are an important part of the church today.

What We Offer

Bible Study & Discipleship

Weekly Bible studies designed specifically for teens, helping them understand and apply God's Word to their daily lives.

Worship & Music

Opportunities for youth to express their faith through contemporary worship and develop their musical talents.

Community Service

Regular service projects that allow youth to put their faith into action by serving those in need in our community.

Social Activities & Retreats

Fun events, game nights, retreats, and camps that foster friendship and create lasting memories.
