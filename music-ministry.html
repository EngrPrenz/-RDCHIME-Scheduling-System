About Our Music Ministry

The Music Ministry at JA1 Church is dedicated to leading the congregation in worship through music that glorifies God and creates an atmosphere where people can encounter His presence. We believe that music is a powerful tool for expressing our love and devotion to God, and for preparing hearts to receive His Word.

Our team consists of vocalists, instrumentalists, sound engineers, and visual technicians who work together to create a seamless worship experience. We are committed to excellence in our musical offerings, while maintaining hearts of humility and service.

Worship Teams & Ensembles

Main Worship Team

Our primary worship team leads the congregation during Sunday services with contemporary worship music that inspires and uplifts.

Church Choir

Our choir performs special musical selections for holidays, special events, and occasionally during Sunday services.

Youth Worship Team

Our youth-led worship team ministers to the youth group and occasionally leads worship for the main congregation.

Technical Team

Our sound, lighting, and visual technicians work behind the scenes to ensure a seamless worship experience.

Our Instruments

We use a variety of instruments to create a rich and dynamic worship experience.

Piano/Keyboard

The foundation of our worship sound.

Acoustic Guitar

Adds warmth and texture to our worship.

Electric Guitar

Provides lead lines and atmospheric elements.

Drums

Drives the rhythm and energy of our worship.

Bass Guitar

Provides the foundational low end for our music.

Violin

Adds beautiful melodic elements to special pieces.
