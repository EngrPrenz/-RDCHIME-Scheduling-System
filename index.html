Welcome to
RDCHIME Scheduling System

RDChime Scheduling System is a platform designed to help organize and manage activities within the Regional Design and Creativity Hub for Innovation of MSMEs in Education. It supports smooth scheduling of workshops, trainings, meetings, and creative sessions. The system aims to promote collaboration, enhance productivity, and provide an efficient way for users to stay informed and connected within the innovation hub.

RDCHIME

RDCHIME

     The Regional Design and Creativity Hub for Innovation, MSMEs and Education (RDCHIME) is a collaborative project between the University of Rizal System and DOST-CALABARZON which aims to

(1) enhance the capability of industries;
(2) train faculty, students, and industry partners in the development of innovative design and creative skills in CALABARZON;
(3) provide educational benefits for the students.

Head of the RDCHIME
